====== Technical support ======

Sympa authors can't answer to any assistance request.  Please check available ressources :

  - [[:manual|reference manual]].
  - [[:faq|faq]]
  -  mailing lists :   Of course, there are also  a few [[http://listes.renater.fr/wws/lists/informatique/sympa|mailing lists about Sympa]]. Please do not report any trouble in lists without having first a look in lists archives. If you contact some mailing list please check first Sympa and other usefull logs such as Apache, LDAP, MySQL or sendmail.
