====== Give us feedback! ======

The informations given by Sympa users are invaluable to us, as they are our main sources of guidelines to improve Sympa.

You can submit issues and pull requests to the project on GitHub:
  * [[https://github.com/sympa-community/sympa | sympa project on GitHub]]

If you have difficulty accessing GitHub site, you may send bug reports to the developers:
  * [[https://listes.renater.fr/sympa/info/sympa-developpers | sympa-developpers mailing list]]  
 Note that your message will be open to the public.

Thank you for your feedback!

The Sympa authors
